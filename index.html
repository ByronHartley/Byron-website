Locking

This style features distinctive, rapid arm and hand movements combined with sudden pauses or “locks” in the dancer’s body. It emphasizes rhythm and groove and was popularized by dancers like Don Campbell.

Popping

Involves contracting and relaxing muscles to create a jerking effect or "pop" in the body. This style emphasizes sharp, isolated movements and often includes intricate footwork and illusions.
